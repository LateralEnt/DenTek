About

Who are we?

DenTek is a group of dental practices in Ukraine offering a wide range of dental services. Our main profile are dental restorations of any kind - quickly and cheaply

Our competent and experienced staff at DenTek utilizing the most modern equipment offer high quality dental treatment to all our clients. Individual treatment planning combined with the use of the latest techniques allows for both effectiveness and flexibility.

Choosing the right dentist can save you quite a lot of money. Due to the low operating costs we can provide our services at prices twice or even three times lower than overseas without compromising quality.

Unlike with other health service providers, at Dentek there is no queue, no waiting list. Whenever you need an appointment or any information just let us know. An email suffices, as does a call or a fax.

Where are we? 

DenTek has practices in Kiev, Odessa and Dnipro - all of which are served with direct flight from Tel Aviv.

In addition to dental treatments our service also includes free airport transfer and accomodation.

Why choose Ukraine for dental treatment?

Private dental offices in Ukraine are equipped with state of the art technology owing to the international clients who have been visiting the country for over many years.

DenTek is also one of them.

Ukrainian dentists are trained to cover all aspects of general dentistry. The Ukrainian Medical Dental/Stomatological Academy (PSMU) is recognized and listed in the International Medical Education Directory (IMED) and is recognised by the World Health Organization. Many Ukrainian dentists work internationally.

Is it really that cheap?

Ukraine offers a wide range of excellent dental facilities which visitors can take advantage of at a much lower price than in Israel.

Indeed, enormous savings can be made by choosing to have your dental work done in our clinic. It can cost less than 50% of the same treatment in Israel. This enables us to offer our patients high quality treatment at an affordable, often amazingly low price.

This is in a great part due to the lower operating costs and wages whereas the applied materials are the same well known brands used all over the world.
