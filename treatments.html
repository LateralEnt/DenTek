Dental restorations

Heavily damaged or missing teeth can be replaced in countless ways using different techniques. They all serve the same purpose though: to reinstate the original function and appearance of the teeth.

Dental implants

Implants have been used in dentistry for many years now. It is a well established technique that opened a whole new world of prosthodontic treatments thereby increasing the number of available treatment options for the patients.

Dental Surgery

Dental surgery deals with illnesses of the teeth, the jaw bone and the surrounding soft tissues that require surgical treatment. These range from simple tooth extraction to the more complex ones like osteotomy or root tip resection.
